Profile

I am qualified in both France and England. I first gained my expertise in France.

After my post graduate study in alternative dispute resolution in 2001 I was the Justice Assistant of a President in the family law division of the Court of Appeal in Paris. I gained valuable experience in ruling and explaining the reasons for a decision.

I have been registered on the Paris French roll since 2005. I trained in Paris with Veronique Chauveau, an expert in international family law and I was then an associate lawyer for BWG and partners, a family law firm in Paris.

I moved to London in 2006 and while starting my English training I carried on working on French matters.

In England I first worked with Michael Nicholls at One Hare Court chambers in international children matters. I was then the associate of Anne-Marie Hutchinson OBE at Dawson Cornwell, a law firm specialising in international family matters. I was admitted on the English roll of Solicitors in 2009.

I joined Elizabeth Muirhead as one of her consultants in 2009 to give advice and act on all aspects of family law and not only children issues. Elizabeth Muirhead is a mediator and a collaborative lawyer and has 40 years of practice in family law matters.

I work with Elizabeth Muirhead on English matters along with Counsel when needed. On French cases I work on my own and represent clients in front of French Courts.
